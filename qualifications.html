Qualifications

We seek individuals who:

  • Are passionate about Sigma Lambda Beta’s history, mission, and values
  • Are enrolled at a college or university where there is an active SLB collegiate entity

Academic Requirements

  • Minimum 2.50 cumulative GPA at time of application
  • First-semester students must show a 3.0 unweighted high school GPA (4.0 scale)

Participation Requirements

  • Attend one informational meeting hosted by the entity you wish to join
  • Attend one MGC informational meeting

Membership is only available to collegiate men who have received an official bid by a collegiate entity of Sigma Lambda Beta.

*For questions regarding qualifications, please reach out.
